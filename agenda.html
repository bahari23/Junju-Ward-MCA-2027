10-Point Plan

My Agenda for Junju Ward

Ten concrete pillars — each backed by specific plans and measurable commitments.

🎯

Youth Employment & Empowerment

Investing in Junju's greatest asset — its young people.

🛠️
Skills Development

Establish vocational training programmes in carpentry, masonry, tailoring, ICT and mechanics within Junju Ward.

💼
Business Grants

Facilitate access to county-level startup grants and micro-finance for young entrepreneurs in Junju.

💡
Youth Innovation Hubs

Lobby for a dedicated Junju Youth Innovation Hub equipped with computers, internet and mentorship resources.

🤝
Internship Opportunities

Partner with county departments and private sector to create structured internship placements for Junju youth.

⚽
Sports Development

Support construction of a youth sports ground and equip local teams for inter-ward competitions.

Commitment Level85%
💧

Clean & Safe Water

Every household in Junju deserves reliable access to clean water.

⛏️
Borehole Projects

Initiate drilling of new boreholes in underserved villages within the first year of taking office.

🔧
Pipeline Expansion

Advocate for extension of the main water pipeline network to reach all corners of Junju Ward.

🏗️
Community Water Tanks

Install elevated storage tanks at strategic points across the ward to ensure water availability.

🌿
Water Conservation

Promote rainwater harvesting and watershed protection to safeguard Junju's long-term water supply.

Commitment Level90%
🛣️

Roads & Infrastructure

Connecting Junju Ward to opportunity through better roads.

🚧
Road Maintenance

Lobby for grading and murram maintenance of all ward access roads on a regular schedule.

💡
Street Lighting

Install solar-powered street lights on major roads and market centres to improve safety.

🌊
Drainage Systems

Construct proper drainage channels to prevent flooding and road damage during rains.

♿
Accessibility

Ensure roads and public spaces are accessible to people with disabilities and the elderly.

Commitment Level75%
📚

Education for All

No child in Junju Ward should miss school due to poverty or poor infrastructure.

🎓
Bursaries

Ensure ward-level bursaries reach needy students in secondary and tertiary institutions from Junju.

🧒
ECDE Support

Construct and equip Early Childhood Development centres and advocate for adequate staffing.

🔩
Vocational Training

Establish a ward-level vocational training centre for school leavers and out-of-school youth.

💻
Digital Learning

Equip schools with computers and internet access to prepare learners for a digital economy.

🏫
School Infrastructure

Prioritise construction of classrooms, libraries and sanitation facilities in Junju's public schools.

Commitment Level88%
🏥

Better Healthcare

Healthy families build a stronger Junju Ward.

🏗️
Health Facility Improvement

Advocate for renovation and equipping of the ward's dispensaries and health centres.

💊
Medicine Availability

Lobby for consistent medicine supply at all public health facilities in Junju Ward.

👶
Maternal Healthcare

Support maternal health programmes including skilled birth attendance and antenatal care outreach.

🩺
Community Health Outreach

Coordinate regular community health camps for screening, immunisation and health education.

Commitment Level80%
👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

Empowered women build a stronger Junju for all.

💼
Women's Business Support

Facilitate access to county women's enterprise funds and market opportunities for female entrepreneurs.

💰
Financial Literacy

Organise financial literacy workshops to help women manage savings, credit and business finances.

🏆
Leadership Opportunities

Actively support and mentor women to take up leadership roles within the ward and county.

🤝
Self-Help Groups

Strengthen existing women's self-help groups and help them access county-level support and funding.

Commitment Level82%
🌾

Agriculture & Food Security

Feeding Junju through modern, productive farming.

🌱
Modern Farming

Introduce modern farming techniques and connect farmers with county agricultural extension officers.

🚿
Irrigation Projects

Lobby for small-scale irrigation schemes to support year-round food production in Junju.

📖
Farmer Training

Organise regular farmer field days and training sessions on soil management and pest control.

🏪
Market Access

Advocate for construction of a ward-level market centre to give farmers fair prices for their produce.

Commitment Level78%
🌳

Environment & Cleanliness

A clean Junju is a healthy, dignified Junju.

🗑️
Waste Management

Establish a proper waste collection system and designated dumpsites to keep Junju clean.

🌲
Tree Planting

Lead annual tree-planting drives in schools, roadsides and public spaces across the ward.

☀️
Climate Resilience

Promote climate-smart practices and advocate for county resources to protect Junju from drought and floods.

🧹
Community Cleanups

Organise monthly community clean-up events and award the cleanest village annually.

Commitment Level72%
🛡️

Security

Every resident of Junju deserves to feel safe in their home and neighbourhood.

💡
Street Lighting

Fast-track installation of solar street lights in market areas, schools and dark public spaces.

👮
Community Policing

Strengthen nyumba kumi initiatives and liaison with police to improve response to security threats.

🎯
Youth Engagement

Channel at-risk youth into skills training and sports to reduce crime and substance abuse.

🏘️
Safe Neighbourhoods

Work with village elders and residents to identify and address security hotspots across Junju.

Commitment Level76%
⚖️

Accountable Leadership

The people of Junju will always know what their MCA is doing — and why.

📢
Public Participation

Hold quarterly barazas in every village to consult residents on budget priorities and development plans.

📊
Quarterly Reports

Publish and distribute quarterly progress reports on all ward projects to every sub-location.

🚪
Open-Door Policy

Maintain a permanently open office — every Junju resident can walk in and speak to the MCA directly.

💰
Budget Transparency

Post the ward development fund budget and expenditure publicly so every shilling can be accounted for.

Commitment Level95%

Ready to Make Junju Better?

Join us in turning these commitments into reality for every family in Junju Ward.
