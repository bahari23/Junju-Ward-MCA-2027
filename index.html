Our Direction

Vision & Mission

Guided by purpose, driven by community.

🌟

Our Vision

A prosperous, self-reliant Junju Ward where every resident — youth, woman, elder — enjoys equitable access to water, education, healthcare, and economic opportunity, underpinned by transparent, accountable leadership.

🎯

Our Mission

To serve Junju Ward residents with integrity and dedication; to advocate for equitable distribution of county resources; and to build a ward where no one is left behind.

Key Priorities

My Agenda for Junju

Ten pillars of transformation — concrete commitments to every family in Junju Ward.

🎯

Youth Empowerment

Skills training, business grants, innovation hubs and sports development for Junju's young people.

💧

Clean & Safe Water

Boreholes, pipeline expansion, community tanks and water conservation programmes.

🛣️

Roads & Infrastructure

Road maintenance, street lighting, drainage systems and improved accessibility.

📚

Education for All

Bursaries, ECDE support, vocational training, digital learning and school infrastructure.

🏥

Better Healthcare

Improved health facilities, medicine availability, maternal care and community outreach.

👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

Business support, financial literacy, leadership opportunities and self-help groups.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Donald Bahari Charo the best candidate for Junju Ward? +

Donald has deep roots in Junju Ward, a proven track record of community service, and a concrete 10-point agenda addressing the ward's most pressing needs — water, youth empowerment, education and healthcare.

What is his position on water access for Junju Ward? +

Access to clean water is priority number one. Donald commits to initiating borehole drilling, expanding water pipelines to underserved areas, and installing community water tanks within the first year in office.

How will he support youth employment? +

Through skills development centres, business startup grants, partnerships with county government for internships, and the establishment of a Junju Youth Innovation Hub.

How can I join the campaign? +

Visit the Volunteer page on this website, fill in the form, and our campaign coordinator will reach out to you within 48 hours. You can also call or WhatsApp our campaign office directly.
